## Working Thesis (to refine when paragraphs are written)

The best-supported theory is that Area 51 sightings reflect **advanced classified human aerospace technology combined with deliberate government misinformation** — not extraterrestrial activity. AARO's 2024 historical review (Tingley), the CIA's 2013 admission (Leonard), and the still-unidentified Dorito-shaped aircraft sightings (Cenciotti) all point to secret aircraft programs being mistaken for or intentionally framed as UFO events.
